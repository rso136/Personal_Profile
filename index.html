About

In the spring of 2016, following ten years of service in the ministry, I decided to take a first step in achieving my goal of becoming a bi-vocational pastor and pursued an education in web development. What unites what would otherwise seem like two disparate vocations is my desire to serve my community in a way that is not self-seeking but rather in a way that is genuinely looking out for the best interest of others.

I am convinced that having a diverse background helps a person to process information from a variety of angles and enables an individual to think outside the box. Coming from a background in technology, ministerial studies, and liberal arts, I have developed a sensitivity and inquisitiveness to a broad spectrum of interests and concerns which reflects the actual complexity of the world around us. In conjunction with my technical proficiencies, I am capable of adapting and thriving in a dynamic environment.

Portfolio Highlight: Destination

Update 1/12/17: I recently developed this Node application to primarily showcase AngularJS proficiency and API integration. Combining the streamlined programming potency of Angular and the rich, dynamic content accessible through a variety of API services, this app demonstrates the core features of modern web development.

By integrating APIs available through Bing Traffic, Open Weather Map, and Eventful, I consolidated instant, real time traffic, weather, and event information for any destination within the U.S. which the user can access through a simple search query. Feel free to check it out!
