Portfolio Highlight: Briarwood Deli

Update 4/15/17: Over the past several weeks, I had the opportunity to put my first freelance commercial project into production. Briarwood Deli is a successful small business deli located in Hamilton, NJ that was looking for a basic mobile responsive website with online ordering integration. From a design standpoint, I decided to go with a rustic aesthetic matching the overall down-to-earth colors and overall feel of the actual business. I chose to go with Gloria Food as the online ordering system for its excellent ease of use and its versatile freemium business model. On a personal note, I was very excited when the first online orders were placed and I was glad to be of practical benefit to a bustling deli with delicious food. If you are ever in the vicinity of Hamilton, NJ and are looking for a quick bite to eat, check them out! The key technologies integrated into this project are as follows:

  • Bootstrap Mobile Responsive Design
  • HTML & CSS
  • Gloria Food Online Ordering System
